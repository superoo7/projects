Our  visions

"VISION IS THE ART OF SEEING WHAT IS INVISIBLE TO OTHERS."

Our group has a vision to build a text to Braille translator that is able to receive incoming text messages via a Bluetooth terminal, which will then process the received information and translate it into the Braille tactile writing system by using a surface with the concept of a braille cell and push type solenoids with Braille pins under the surface to make Braille texts on the surface. This project is designed for us to communicate with people with impaired hearing and vision more easily.
